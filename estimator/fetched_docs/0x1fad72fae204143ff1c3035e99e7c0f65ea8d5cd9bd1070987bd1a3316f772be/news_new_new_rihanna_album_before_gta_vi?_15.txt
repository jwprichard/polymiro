Title: Rihanna Posts Documentary Proof She Is Recording Music
URL: https://www.rollingstone.com/music/music-news/rihanna-recording-new-album-video-recording-studio-1235522707/

Rihanna, who has said she’s been working on her first new album in a decade, has now shared some cold, hard proof that she really is in the recording studio. Just before 2 a.m., Rihanna announces she must go to the studio to record a little more of her upcoming, as-yet-unofficially-unannounced ninth album, after which she needs to make a Mardi Gras costume for her son. Rihanna then meets her studio staff, who record with her until 5:05 a.m., though there’s no audio in this portion of the clip she’s willing to share at this point.

# Rihanna confirms new music – and reveals why she kept fans waiting. New music will be warmly welcomed by the singer’s significant fan base; not only is Rihanna the best-selling female recording artist of the 21st century, with nine Grammy awards to her name, she also managed to shift one million albums in 2024 alone (despite not releasing any new material). Rihanna’s efforts to create new music saw her re-listen to *Anti*, which has since helped her shape the direction that she wants *R9* to go in. In an exclusive interview for *Harper’s Bazaar*, our April cover star says: “I think music is my freedom. I know I kept saying this over the years.”. While it was previously rumoured that Rihanna’s stylistic direction for the new album could involve reggae, she dismisses any suggestion of genre – and is insisting she will only put the music out when she is ready.
